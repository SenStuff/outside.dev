AI Development

[[TIMESTAMP: 05.07.25]] [[AUTHOR: yaragirodev]]

Today I want to tell you about the new AI system for the monster named "Harry".


The core logic is simple and practical, though some bugs occur due to Roblox Raycasting, which I'm actively fixing.


Harry follows a path built from named parts like Waypoint1, Waypoint2, etc. He moves like this:

Spawn Point ➔ Waypoint1 ➔ Waypoint2 ➔ ...


After reaching the last waypoint, he goes back to the first one and repeats. Raycasting helps him navigate between points.

If a player appears in his field of vision, Harry begins chasing. If the player escapes, Harry searches the last known position.


What he can do?


    - He can climb ladders

    - He can jumb (but bad)
