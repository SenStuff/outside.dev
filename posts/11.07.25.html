11.07.25 New Updates - New Location, New Weather

[[TIMESTAMP: 11.07.25]] [[AUTHOR: yaragirodev]]

Hello everyone!


Today we’re excited to share what’s new in our game!


Map Updates

We’ve made several changes to the map:


    A new location has been added — The Outskirts, featuring an electric power station.


    Streetlights across the map now automatically turn off during the day, making the world feel more alive and realistic.


New Weather

    We’ve introduced a new weather type — rain!


    Now, you might experience a real downpour during gameplay, adding more atmosphere and variety to your adventure.
