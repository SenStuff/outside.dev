PROJECT OVERVIEW

What is "The Outside"?

"The Outside" is an immersive multiplayer horror experience set in the abandoned city of Blackstone. Players must work together to survive encounters with a mysterious entity known as "Harry" while uncovering the dark secrets of a failed research project. Combining psychological horror with cooperative gameplay, the experience challenges players to balance survival instincts with scientific curiosity.

THE BLACKSTONE INCIDENT

Background Lore

In 2019, the Blackstone Research Facility was conducting classified experiments on dimensional anomalies. The project, codenamed "OUTSIDE," aimed to create controlled breaches in reality for scientific observation. However, something went wrong during Test Subject H-4RRY...


The city was evacuated within hours. Official reports cited a "chemical leak," but survivors tell a different story. They speak of shadows that move independently, of whispers in empty rooms, and of something that watches from the spaces between spaces.


Now, years later, research teams are being sent back into Blackstone. Their mission: document the anomaly, study its behavior, and determine if containment is possible. But in Blackstone, the observer becomes the observed, and the hunter becomes the hunted.
